# sales-analysis
this is Sales analysis project showing product performance, revenue trends, and customer insights using data visualization to improve business decisions
Project Overview

This project provides a detailed analysis of Adidas sales data to understand business performance, revenue trends, and customer purchasing behavior. The analysis aims to generate actionable insights that can support data-driven decisions for marketing, production, and sales strateg

Objectives

To analyze overall sales performance and revenue distribution across regions and product categories.

To identify top-performing products and their contribution to total revenue.

To examine seasonal and regional sales trends.

To provide actionable insights for improving future sales strategies.

Methodology

Data Collection & Cleaning: Gathered Adidas sales dataset and performed data cleaning to remove duplicates, missing values, and inconsistencies.

Data Preparation: Formatted data into structured tables suitable for analysis and visualization.

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Used pivot tables, charts, and statistical summaries to explore relationships and trends in the dataset.

Visualization: Created interactive dashboards highlighting key performance indicators (KPIs) such as revenue, profit, quantity sold, and regional performance.

Interpretation: Derived insights from patterns observed in the data visualizations and analysis results.

Key Insights

Footwear and apparel contributed the highest share of total revenue.

The Western region recorded the highest sales performance compared to other regions.

Online sales channels showed steady growth over the analyzed period.

Seasonal patterns indicated peak sales during the third quarter, aligning with promotional campaigns.

High-value customers were concentrated in major urban locations, driving overall revenue growth.

Conclusion

The Adidas sales analysis revealed valuable insights into customer preferences, top-performing regions, and seasonal sales behavior. These findings can help optimize marketing efforts, enhance inventory management, and improve overall business strategies. The project demonstrates the power of data analysis in guiding informed business decisions and evaluating market performance
